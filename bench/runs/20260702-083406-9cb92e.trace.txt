I chose to use a dictionary `num_counts` to keep track of the occurrences of each number. As I iterate through the list, I increment the count for each number. After populating the dictionary, I sum up the counts that are exactly 1 to get the final result.

This approach ensures that all unique numbers (those with exactly one occurrence) are counted correctly, and it handles an edge case where the input list is empty by returning 0 immediately.
