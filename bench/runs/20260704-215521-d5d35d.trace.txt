The function sorts the list of numbers and removes both minimum and maximum occurrences. If the number of remaining elements is even, it calculates the mean by summing all the remaining numbers and dividing by the count of remaining numbers. Otherwise, it calculates the mean by summing the first half plus one middle number (if any) and dividing by the total count of the remaining numbers. An edge case to handle an empty list is also included, returning None in such a scenario.
