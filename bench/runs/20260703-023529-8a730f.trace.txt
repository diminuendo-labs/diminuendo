I chose to use a stack-based approach to solve this problem, as it is well-suited for handling nested structures like parentheses. By iterating over the string and pushing opening parentheses onto the stack and popping them off when a closing parenthesis is encountered, I can efficiently determine whether the parentheses are balanced or not. The edge case of an unmatched closing parenthesis is handled by checking if the stack is empty before attempting to pop from it.
