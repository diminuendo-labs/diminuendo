I implemented the function `median` by first checking if the input list is empty and returning `None` in that case, as required. Then I sorted the list using the built-in `sorted` function. Next, I determined whether the length of the sorted list was odd or even by checking if the remainder when divided by 2 was 1 or 0. For odd lengths, I returned the middle element; for even lengths, I calculated and returned the average of the two middle elements. I chose to use a simple and readable approach with clear variable names and comments to explain the logic behind the function.
