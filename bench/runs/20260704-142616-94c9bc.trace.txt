** The function first checks if the input list `words` is empty or if `k` is out of range. If so, it returns `None`. It then groups words by their length and sorts each group in reverse-alphabetical order. After that, it flattens the dictionary into a single list of ordered words and returns the k-th word (adjusting for 1-based indexing). The edge cases handled are when `k` is negative or greater than the number of words in the list.
