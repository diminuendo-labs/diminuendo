** I used a list comprehension to iterate through the list in chunks of size `k`. For each chunk, if its length is less than `k`, I padded it with `None` by calculating the difference between `k` and the current chunk's length and appending that many `None`s. This ensures every chunk has exactly `k` elements, even if the last chunk is shorter than `k`. The function handles an empty list by returning an empty list as requested.
