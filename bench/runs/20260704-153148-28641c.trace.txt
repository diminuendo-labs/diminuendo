I solved this task by using Python's built-in `sorted` function with a custom sorting key. The key is a tuple where the first element is the length of the word and the second element is the negative lexicographical order (to reverse it). This ensures that words are ordered by increasing length, and when lengths are equal, they are ordered reverse-alphabetically among themselves. I also added an edge case to handle the situation where there are no words in the input list.
