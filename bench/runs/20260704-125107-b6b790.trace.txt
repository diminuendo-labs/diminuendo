To achieve the desired functionality, I first replaced multiple consecutive spaces, commas, and semicolons with their single counterparts to avoid additional splits. Next, I defined a string of all delimiters to be used in the `split()` method. After splitting the string, I removed any empty pieces at the beginning or end using list comprehension. Lastly, I rejoined the pieces with a comma between each piece and returned the resulting string. One edge case this function handles is when there are multiple consecutive delimiters; it will be treated as one split instead of creating extra pieces in the result.
