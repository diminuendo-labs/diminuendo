First, I created a list of tuples with each tuple containing the length and word. Then, I sorted this list by first increasing the length and then decreasing the alphabetical order among words of equal length. Next, I used a generator expression to find the k-th word in the sorted list. The edge case I handled is when k exceeds the number of words available in the list (i.e., k > len(words)). In this case, I return None instead of raising an exception or throwing off the indexing.
