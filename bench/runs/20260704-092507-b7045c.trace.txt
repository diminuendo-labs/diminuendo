First, I split the input string by underscores to separate each word in the snake_case identifier. Then, I iterate through the list of words and for each word, I concatenate it with the previous word (if any) using lowercase for the first word and capitalizing subsequent words. To handle edge cases, leading and trailing underscores are discarded by only taking into account words that are separated by an underscore. Additionally, consecutive underscores are replaced with a single underscore before splitting them to prevent creating extra words.
