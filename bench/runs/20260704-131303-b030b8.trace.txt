The task requires ordering words based on their length and alphabetically for equal lengths. I used a custom sorting function with a key function `lambda word: (len(word), word)`, which first sorts by length, then by the actual alphabetical order. The requested word is returned if it's within the range, otherwise None is returned to handle out-of-range cases. An edge case considered here is when there are fewer than k words in the list (k > len(words)).
