The approach is to first replace all underscores (_) with nothing, then split the string into individual words based on spaces (since underscores were removed). If there are any words, we capitalize the first letter of each word after the first and join them together, ensuring that the first word starts in lowercase. This will handle both the normal case of snake_case identifiers as well as edge cases where leading or trailing underscores are present, such as '__a__b_' which becomes 'aB'. An empty string is returned when there are no words to process.
