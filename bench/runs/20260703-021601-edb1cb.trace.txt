** I approached the task by using Python's string `split` method to break down the input string into a list of words based on underscores. Then, I used a generator expression within the `join` method to capitalize each word and concatenate them together. The filter condition in the generator ensures that any empty strings (resulting from leading/trailing/continuous underscores) are not included in the final result. This solution is concise and readable, adhering to Pythonic conventions.
