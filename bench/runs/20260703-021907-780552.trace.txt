**
The function `snake_to_camel` takes a string in snake_case format, splits it by underscores, filters out any empty strings (which can occur due to leading/trailing or repeated underscores), and then capitalizes the first letter of each resulting substring. The substrings are then joined together without any separators to form a camelCase string. This approach ensures that the function is concise and readable while handling edge cases like leading, trailing, and repeated underscores appropriately.
