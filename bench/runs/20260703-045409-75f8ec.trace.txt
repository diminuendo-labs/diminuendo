I approached this problem by first sorting the list of numbers to ensure they are in ascending order. I then iterated through the sorted list, keeping track of the start and end of consecutive sequences. When a non-consecutive number is encountered, I appended the current sequence to the result list as either a single number or a range, depending on whether it was consecutive or not. After the loop, I made sure to append the last sequence. Finally, I joined all elements in the result list with commas to form the final string. This approach handles the edge case of an empty list by immediately returning an empty string.
