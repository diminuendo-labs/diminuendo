I approached this problem by first splitting the input string into individual words, and then iterating over each word to determine whether it needs title-casing. If a word is already entirely uppercase, I left it as is; otherwise, I applied title-casing using Python's built-in `title()` method. Finally, I joined the processed words back together with single spaces in between to produce the final result. This solution handles the edge case of words already entirely uppercase, and aims for conciseness and readability by keeping each step separate and well-identified.
