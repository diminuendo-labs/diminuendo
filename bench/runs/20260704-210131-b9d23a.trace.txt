I chose to first handle the edge case of an empty or partially filled list by returning None. Then I sorted the input list and determined the median value, which would be the middle element when the list has an odd length or the average of the two middle elements for even-length lists. After finding the left cutoff (the second smallest number) and right cutoff (the second largest number), I calculated the sum of the remaining numbers in the list after excluding these extremes, divided by the total count minus 2 to find the trimmed mean.
