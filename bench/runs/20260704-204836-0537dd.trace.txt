First, I sorted the list in ascending order. Then, I determined the indices of the minimum and maximum values by finding the middle index and calculating the indices for the minimum and maximum occurrences. Next, I created a new list that excluded the first and last minimum/maximum values. Finally, I calculated the mean using the total sum of the trimmed numbers and the new length of the list (excluding the two dropped values). An edge case considered is when the input list is empty, in which case the function returns None.
