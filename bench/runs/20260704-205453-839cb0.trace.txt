The function `trimmed_mean(nums)` calculates the mean of a list of numbers after removing exactly one minimum and one maximum value. First, it sorts the input list in ascending order. Then, if the list has 2 or fewer elements, it returns None as per the specified edge case. Finally, it sums all the elements excluding both ends (minimum and maximum) and divides by the number of remaining elements minus 2 or 4 (depending on whether the list contains less than or more than 4 elements), thereby providing the trimmed mean of the given list.
