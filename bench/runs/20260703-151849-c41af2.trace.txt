I then iterated through the sorted list, identifying consecutive runs. For each run, if there was more than one number, I added a range to the result in the 'start-end' format; otherwise, I added the single number. Finally, I joined all parts of the result with commas and returned the string. This approach handles edge cases like an empty list and lists with only one or two elements efficiently.
