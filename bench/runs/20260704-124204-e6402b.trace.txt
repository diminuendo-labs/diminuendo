I first replaced commas and semicolons with newline characters to simplify the splitting process. Then, I split the string on newlines, removing any empty pieces that may have resulted from consecutive delimiters. I kept only the non-empty pieces, as empty pieces at the start or end would not affect the overall result but are unnecessary to include. Finally, I rejoined the remaining pieces with a single comma between each. The edge case I handled is the presence of multiple consecutive delimiters in any combination (e.g., `a,;;b`).
